Welcome to the ASEAN Youth Forum

An innovative platform to strengthen the ASEAN youth community in dialogue, collaboration, and innovation for a better future

Our Programs

Conferences and Seminars

This program allows youth from across ASEAN to come together and discuss relevant and useful topics. Conferences or seminars can discuss various topics such as political, economic, social, environmental and cultural issues.

Workshops and Training

This program provides opportunities for participants to gain new skills and knowledge in certain fields, such as leadership, entrepreneurship, application development, and so on.

Competition

This program provides an opportunity for participants to participate in competitions related to relevant and useful issues. These competitions can be in the form of technology competitions, social innovation, writing, and so on.

Social activities

This program provides opportunities for participants to carry out positive social activities, such as visits to orphanages, environmental activities, or fundraising for certain diseases.

Arts and Culture Performances

This program provides an opportunity for participants to express their creativity and demonstrate their unique culture through art and cultural performances such as music, dance, drama and fine arts.

Field trip

This program provides opportunities for participants to visit places that are relevant to the topics discussed in the forum, such as technology companies, research centers, or tourism sites.
